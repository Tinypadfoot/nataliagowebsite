Natalia Go is a Filipino speculative science fiction writer living in the US. Her works include Interfinity and Vitality published on Amazon Kindle. She is fond of writing stories with lesbian protagonists in the near-future setting. She likes to imagine progressive worlds where humanity is not held back by religion. Outside of her writing career, she is a theatre actor and a budding web developer.
