Fernan-Vaz Gorilla Project

Gorillas are under threat of extinction because of the bushmeat trade and poaching!

I am an offsite volunteer for a gorilla sanctuary located in Gabon, Central Africa. I help the organization manage their social media pages and contribute website content from my current home in the US. Through storytelling, we are able to raise awareness on the importance of gorilla conservation and its human dimension. I love gorillas! They are intelligent and gentle creatures and they need our help.

Learn more about gorilla conservation and what nonprofit organizations like ours are doing in order to protect them on our Facebook Page
