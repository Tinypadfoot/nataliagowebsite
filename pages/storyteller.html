On a planet covered entirely by water, a new civilization grew on manmade islands, underwater districts, and flying cities. With the new government in place, technology is thriving but the arts is a little way behind. Lilah, the archetype of the new world's performing artist is ready to give up on her acting career when a strange girl shows up in her library one night and lures her into a dimension where imagined characters come to life. In this world in between worlds, she discovers a force that would transform the future of theatre. This is the story of Lilah and the Suspension of Disbelief. A sci-fi fantasy novel in the works of being published.
