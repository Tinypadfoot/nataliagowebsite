Planet Avion is covered entirely by water. When humans saw it fit to terraform and inhabit, they built manmade islands, underwater districts, and flying cities. With the new government in place, technology is advancing but art is dying. Lilah, an unsuccessful performing artist meets new friends who would help her transform the future of theatre in this strange new world. This is the story of Lilah and the Suspension of Disbelief. A sci-fi fantasy novel in the works of being published .
